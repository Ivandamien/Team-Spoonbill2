Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Favicon?

Favicons are known as favorite icons, URL icons, or website icons. They are very small icons (usually 16x16 or 32x32) and appear nect to your website URL in browser tabs and bookmarks, making it easy for your visitors to indentify your website in their tabs.

Why Should I Use the Favicon Generator?

Instead of having to manually resize your website logo to work as a favicon, our Favicon Generator instantly provides you with a correctly sized favicon in ICO format. Having a favicon is really important for branding and with our Favicon Generator, yoy have no excuse not to have a perfectly scaled icon to make your website easily identifiable.

How Do I Add a Favicon to My Website?

The way you add Favicon to your website depends on the platform that you use. For example, if you use Wordpress, you can upload a favicon in the Theme Customizer (in the 'site identity' section, usually). Other website builders also have a simple favicon upload function. If youwant to manually insert the code for your favicon , you will need to upload the icon you've created to your website. Then, to check it's uploaded correctly, type hhtps://websitename.com/favicon.png into your browser. You will then need to add this HTML code for the favicon in the HEAD dection of your pages and the main index page of your website: <link rel="shortcut icon" href="https://example.com/favicon.png"/> If you use website caching, you will need to purge the cache before your favicon will show up.

Why do I need an ICO file instead of a PNG?

An ICO file is a special image file used by the browser. The unique feature of an ICO file is that it is multilayered. Each layer of the favicon holds a differnet size of the image. The common sizes of a ICO formatted favicon are 16 x 16px, 32 x 32px, and 48 x 48px. For best compatibility web browsers can leverage the ICO file generated by the favicon generator. The browsers will use the differnet sizes for displaying in different areas of the website such as the bookmarks bar, the address bar, the browser tab and as a desktop shortcut.

What types of images work best for the favicon generator

The favicon generator works best with a simple icon, logo or letter. Intricate or complex designs don't work well when they are resized using the favicon generator as much detail is lost. If your logo is very complex we recommend generating the favicon from text using the alternative generator.
